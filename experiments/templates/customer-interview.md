## Background Questions
1. Tell me about your role and responsibilities.
2. How long have you been doing this?
3. What does a typical day/week look like?

## Problem Exploration
4. What's the most frustrating part of [DOMAIN AREA]?
5. How often does this happen?
6. Can you walk me through the last time this happened?
7. What did you do to solve it?
8. How much time/money did that cost?

## Current Solutions
9. What tools do you currently use for this?
10. What do you like about them?
11. What do you wish was different?
12. Have you tried other solutions? Why did you switch/stay?

## Budget & Decision
13. Who else is involved in decisions like this?
14. What's your budget for tools in this area?
15. What would make you switch from your current solution?

## Feature Validation
16. If I could [SOLUTION DESCRIPTION], how valuable would that be? (1-10)
17. What would make it a 10?
18. What's the one thing it absolutely must do?

## Closing
19. Is there anyone else I should talk to about this?
20. Can I follow up with you as we build this?
